Inspections

All lifting equipment must have a current “Report of Thorough Examination” when in use. Principle Lifting and Safety can inspect your equipment on site or at our premises. Ask us for advice on compliance and ensuring you remain compliant. You have the option to bring your equipment to our depot or arrange an inspection at your own premises. Our on-site service ensures your equipment is safe to operate and remains compliant with current legislation in a cost effective manner with minimal disruption to your business. If any examination reveals the need for repair, we will provide a detailed quotation. If the repair of an item of equipment is not viable, or uneconomical, we can provide replacements parts at competitively priced rates.
